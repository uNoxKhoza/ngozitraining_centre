ABOUT US

NGOZIDHLAMINI is a 100% black owned company striving to train and develop all people in all sorts lives throughout
South Africa and Abroad. Our courses designed and presented by safety oriented Training Officers who have been in the mining,
engineering and construction industries for their whole lives. They know the needs , challenges and hazards in the field. They are just not training proffesionals but they are subject
the matter experts. Our training is not just a bunch of manuals to take home but the learners are encouraged and assisted with visual and practical knowledge in every course. We strive to offer quality education and training in all our courses and we do not allow our competitive prices to temper with the quality of our learning material. If you need to be compliant with the OHSA/MHSA ngozidhlamini training
Centre is just a call away.
